The ocean is vast and unknown. To me a website is a lot like that, endless possibilities but difficult to grasp.
Like the waves on a shoreline, my website flows in and out. Sometimes a moment of clarity appears and I know
what to do, and other times I'm back in the deep unknown.
Moments of creative inspiration and then a lot of floating.
A website connects you to the world if you want it to, just like the ocean.
